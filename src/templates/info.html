Hello and welcome to my sample website. It shows a list of asteroids that are predicted to fly near earth on a given day. It's not like we have enough to worry about, right?

Who am I?

My name is Jeremy Yung and I am an IT worker in the San Francisco Bay Area. My primary focus is to support the backend infrastructure for development teams, by whatever means necessary.

How did I make this site?

I started out by using the default Docker container for Apache. Then, I configured it to serve up a Python FLASK website. I then deployed it on Amazon's EC2 service, since it is very common.(i.e. cheap) All of the data is being pulled from NASA's Near Earth Object(NEO) API and then filtered by today's date.

Why did I make this?

I wanted to familiarize myself with the process of deploying a website, starting at the very beginning. It's a simple website that I plan to improve upon in the future, but it encapsulates the type of work I enjoy doing.
